Prerequities:
-------------

You need CMake to generate the project files for your IDE.

How-to build?
-------------

 . Go this directory and generate the makefile you want by typing in the console
 somethink like this line:
   cmake -G "Unix Makefiles"
 or
   cmake -G "Visual Studio 10"
 
 . Some other options can be accessed by launching something like:
   cmake -i -G "Your generator"
